## Scenario: Self-Checkout Machines That Won't Cooperate

### Your Goal
You're using a self-checkout machine but problems arise: unexpected item error, age verification needed, barcode won't scan, machine only speaks Polish, or you need to call an attendant.

### Setting
You're at a self-checkout station at Biedronka, Kaufland, or another grocery store.

---

## Complication 1: "Unexpected Item" Error

**The machine yells at you.**

**Machine:** Nieoczekiwany przedmiot w strefie pakowania. Proszę zdjąć ostatni produkt. `(nyeh-o-che-KEE-va-nih pshed-MYOT v STRE-fyeh pa-ko-VA-nya. PRO-sheh zdyonch OS-tat-nee pro-DOOKT)` - Unexpected item in bagging area. Please remove the last product.

**You:** *Didn't put anything wrong*

**Your options:**

**Option A - Try removing and re-scanning:**
- *Take item off, wait, put it back*
- Machine might reset

**Option B - Call attendant:**
- **You:** *Press help button*
- **You:** Przepraszam, jest problem. `(psheh-PRA-sham, yest PROB-lem)` - Sorry, there's a problem.

**Option C - Show attendant screen:**
- **You:** *Point at screen* Nie rozumiem. Co mam zrobić? `(nyeh ro-ZOO-myem. tso mam ZRO-beech)` - I don't understand. What should I do?

---

## Complication 2: Age Verification Needed

**You're buying alcohol or certain medicines.**

**Machine:** *Red light flashes* Weryfikacja wieku. Proszę czekać na pracownika. `(ve-rih-fee-KATS-ya VYEH-koo. PRO-sheh CHE-kach na pra-TSOV-nee-ka)` - Age verification. Please wait for an employee.

**You:** *Wait for attendant*

**Attendant:** Dowód? `(do-VOOD)` - ID?

**Your options:**

**Option A - Show ID:**
- **You:** Proszę. `(PRO-sheh)` - Here you go.
- *Hand over ID or passport*

**Option B - Explain you don't have it:**
- **You:** Nie mam przy sobie. `(nyeh mam pshih SO-byeh)` - I don't have it with me.
- **Attendant:** To nie mogę zatwierdzić. `(to nyeh MO-geh zat-VYEHR-jeech)` - Then I can't approve it.

**Option C - Ask if necessary:**
- **You:** Naprawdę potrzeba? Mam czterdzieści lat. `(na-PRAV-deh po-TSHE-ba? mam chter-JESH-chee lat)` - Is it really necessary? I'm forty years old.
- *They might just approve it*

---

## Complication 3: Barcode Won't Scan

**You try scanning but nothing happens.**

**You:** *Wave item over scanner repeatedly*
**Machine:** *Nothing*

**Your options:**

**Option A - Look for manual entry:**
- **You:** *Point at item* Nie skanuje się. `(nyeh ska-NOO-ye sheh)` - It won't scan.
- **Attendant:** *Manually enters code*

**Option B - Find barcode:**
- **You:** Gdzie jest kod kreskowy? `(gjeh yest kot kres-KO-vih)` - Where is the barcode?
- *Maybe it's hidden*

**Option C - Ask for help:**
- **You:** Przepraszam, ten produkt nie skanuje się. `(psheh-PRA-sham, ten pro-DOOKT nyeh ska-NOO-ye sheh)` - Sorry, this product won't scan.

---

## Complication 4: Weighing Produce Problems

**You need to weigh loose fruit/vegetables but the machine is confusing.**

**Machine:** Proszę wybrać produkt. `(PRO-sheh VIH-brach pro-DOOKT)` - Please select product.

**You:** *See long list of Polish vegetable names*

**Your options:**

**Option A - Look for picture:**
- *Tap through menus looking for images*
- **You:** *Point at screen* To? `(to)` - This one?

**Option B - Ask attendant for help:**
- **You:** Przepraszam, szukam pomidorów. `(psheh-PRA-sham, SHOO-kam po-mee-DO-roof)` - Sorry, I'm looking for tomatoes.

**Option C - Use common produce names:**
- Banany, Jabłka, Pomidory, Ogórki `(ba-NA-nih, YAP-ka, po-mee-DO-rih, o-GOOR-kee)` - Bananas, Apples, Tomatoes, Cucumbers

---

## Complication 5: Machine Only Speaks Polish

**No language option visible.**

**Machine:** Proszę włożyć produkt do strefy pakowania. `(PRO-sheh VWO-zhich pro-DOOKT do STRE-fy pa-ko-VA-nya)` - Please place item in bagging area.

**You:** *Completely lost by rapid Polish*

**Your options:**

**Option A - Watch and follow icons:**
- *Usually icons indicate what to do*
- Green = scan, arrows = place item

**Option B - Ask for English:**
- **You:** Czy jest po angielsku? `(chih yest po an-GYEL-skoo)` - Is there English?
- **Attendant:** Nie, tylko po polsku. `(nyeh, TIL-ko po POL-skoo)` - No, only Polish.

**Option C - Ask attendant to help throughout:**
- **You:** Przepraszam, mój polski jest słaby. Może pan/pani pomóc? `(psheh-PRA-sham, mooy POL-skee yest SWA-bih. MO-zheh pan/PA-nee po-MOOTS)` - Sorry, my Polish is weak. Can you help?

---

## Complication 6: Payment Problems

**Card doesn't work or you need cash.**

**Machine:** Płatność odrzucona. Spróbuj ponownie. `(PWAT-noshch od-zhoo-TSO-na. SPROO-buy po-NOV-nyeh)` - Payment declined. Try again.

**Your options:**

**Option A - Try again:**
- **You:** *Try card again, different way*

**Option B - Try different card:**
- **You:** *Use different payment method*

**Option C - Ask about cash:**
- **You:** Czy mogę zapłacić gotówką? `(chih MO-geh za-PWA-cheech go-TOOV-koh)` - Can I pay cash?
- *Some machines take cash, some don't*

**Option D - Ask for help:**
- **You:** Karta nie działa. `(KAR-ta nyeh JA-wa)` - Card isn't working.

---

## Complication 7: Machine Freezes

**Everything stops.**

**Machine:** *Frozen screen or spinning wheel*

**You:** *Wait... wait...*

**Your options:**

**Option A - Call attendant:**
- **You:** *Press help button or wave*
- **You:** Maszyna się zawiesiła. `(ma-SHIH-na sheh za-vyeh-SHEE-wa)` - The machine froze.

**Option B - Point at problem:**
- **You:** *Point at screen* Coś jest nie tak. `(tsosh yest nyeh tak)` - Something is wrong.

**Option C - Ask to move:**
- **You:** Czy mogę przejść do innej kasy? `(chih MO-geh psheyshch do EEN-ney KA-sih)` - Can I go to another checkout?

---

## Complication 8: Bag Needed

**You need a bag but can't figure out how to add one.**

**You:** *Looking for bag option*

**Your options:**

**Option A - Scan bag first:**
- **You:** *Look for bag barcode to scan*
- Usually bags have barcodes near the station

**Option B - Ask attendant:**
- **You:** Jak dodać torbę? `(yak DO-dach TOR-beh)` - How do I add a bag?

**Option C - Find button:**
- **You:** Gdzie jest przycisk \"torba\"? `(gjeh yest PSHIH-chisk TOR-ba)` - Where is the "bag" button?

## Cultural Notes

- **Self-checkout is common**: Most major Polish stores (Biedronka, Kaufland, Lidl, Auchan) have self-checkouts.
- **Attendants are nearby**: There's always someone watching the self-checkout area. Wave or press the help button.
- **ID for alcohol**: Age verification is strict. Have ID ready if buying alcohol.
- **Bag scanning**: Most stores require you to scan the bag barcode before using it.
- **Polish only**: Most self-checkout machines don't have English. Watch the icons.

## Practice Variations

### Intermediate Version
- Call for help: "Przepraszam, jest problem"
- Point at issue: *gesture to screen*
- Thank: "Dziękuję"

### Advanced Version
- Explain problem: "Produkt nie skanuje się i maszyna pokazuje błąd"
- Ask specific question: "Gdzie znajdę przycisk do torby?"
- Handle payment issue: "Karta została odrzucona, czy mogę zapłacić inaczej?"
