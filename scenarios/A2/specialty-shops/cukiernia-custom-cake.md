## Scenario: Ordering Birthday Cake

### Your Goal
You want to order a custom cake for a birthday. You need to describe what you want and arrange pickup.

### Setting
You're at a cukiernia (pastry shop). You want to order a special cake for next week.

---

## Dialogue

### Opening

**You:** Dzień dobry. Chciałbym/Chciałabym zamówić tort. `(jen DOB-rih. HCHAW-bim/HCHAW-abim za-MOO-veech tort)` - Good day. I would like to order a cake.

**Attendant:** Oczywiście! Na kiedy? `(o-chih-VEESH-cheh! na KYEH-dih)` - Of course! For when?

**You:** Na przyszły piątek. `(na PSHIH-shwih PYON-tek)` - For next Friday.

---

### Describing What You Want

**Attendant:** Jaki tort? Ile osób? `(YA-kee tort? EE-leh O-soop)` - What kind of cake? How many people?

**You:** To na urodziny. Dla dziesięciu osób. `(to na oo-ro-JEE-nih. dla jeh-SHEN-choo O-soop)` - It's for a birthday. For ten people.

**Attendant:** Dobrze. Jaki smak? `(DOB-zheh. YA-kee smak)` - Okay. What flavor?

**You:** Czekoladowy. `(che-ko-la-DO-vih)` - Chocolate.

**Alternative flavors:**
- Truskawkowy `(troos-kaf-KO-vih)` - Strawberry
- Waniliowy `(va-nee-LYO-vih)` - Vanilla
- Serowy `(se-RO-vih)` - Cheesecake

---

### Special Requests

**Attendant:** Czy ma być napis? `(chih ma bitch NA-pees)` - Should there be writing?

**You:** Tak. "Sto lat" i imię. `(tak. sto lat ee EE-myeh)` - Yes. "Happy birthday" and a name. *(lit: "Hundred years")*

**You:** Możecie napisać "Sto lat Ania"? `(mo-ZHEH-cheh na-PEE-sach sto lat A-nya)` - Can you write "Happy Birthday Ania"?

**Attendant:** Tak, oczywiście. `(tak, o-chih-VEESH-cheh)` - Yes, of course.

---

### Finalizing Details

**Attendant:** Która godzina odbioru? `(KTOO-ra go-JEE-na od-BYO-roo)` - What time for pickup?

**You:** Czy może być o czternastej? `(chih MO-zheh bitch o chter-NAS-tey)` - Can it be at 2 PM?

**Attendant:** Tak, będzie gotowy. Proszę nazwisko i telefon. `(tak, BEN-jeh go-TO-vih. PRO-sheh naz-VEES-ko ee te-LE-fon)` - Yes, it will be ready. [Your] name and phone, please.

**You:** [Name and phone number]

**Attendant:** Ile będzie kosztować? `(EE-leh BEN-jeh kosh-TO-vach)` - How much will it cost?

**Attendant:** Osiemdziesiąt złotych. Proszę zapłacić teraz połowę. `(o-shem-JEH-shont ZWO-tih. PRO-sheh za-PWA-cheech te-RAS po-WO-veh)` - Eighty złoty. Please pay half now.

**You:** Dobrze. Proszę. `(DOB-zheh. PRO-sheh)` - Okay. Here you go.

---

### Closing

**Attendant:** Dziękuję. Do zobaczenia w piątek. `(jen-KOO-yeh. do zo-ba-CHE-nya v PYON-tek)` - Thank you. See you on Friday.

**You:** Dziękuję bardzo! Do zobaczenia! `(jen-KOO-yeh BAR-dzo! do zo-ba-CHE-nya)` - Thank you very much! See you!

## Cultural Notes

- **"Sto lat"**: Polish "Happy Birthday" means "hundred years" - wishing long life
- **Deposit required**: Many cake shops require 50% deposit when ordering custom cakes
- **Advance notice**: Order at least 2-3 days ahead for custom cakes
- **Name and phone**: They'll need both to find your order and contact you if needed
- **Popular occasions**: Birthdays, name days (imieniny), communions, weddings

---

## Practice Variations

### Intermediate Version
- State purpose: "Chciałbym zamówić tort na urodziny"
- Choose flavor: "Czekoladowy, proszę"
- Arrange pickup: "Na piątek, proszę"

### Advanced Version
- Be specific: "Potrzebuję tortu czekoladowego dla dziesięciu osób z napisem 'Sto lat Ania'" (I need a chocolate cake for ten people with the inscription 'Happy Birthday Ania')
- Ask about options: "Jakie smaki polecacie?" (What flavors do you recommend?)
- Confirm details: "Więc odbiór w piątek o czternastej. Zgadza się?" (So pickup Friday at 2 PM. Is that correct?)
