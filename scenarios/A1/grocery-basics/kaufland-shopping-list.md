## Scenario: Checkout Surprises at Kaufland

### Your Goal
You've got your items and you're at the register. But the cashier will ask questions you might not expect - about bags, loyalty cards, receipts, and payment. Some might be phrased in ways you've never heard.

### Setting
You're at Kaufland checkout with a few items. The cashier is efficient (read: fast). Be ready for rapid questions.

---

## The Basic Flow (When It Goes Smoothly)

**Cashier:** Dzień dobry! `(jen DOB-rih)` - Good day!
**You:** Dzień dobry. `(jen DOB-rih)` - Good day.

*They scan your items*

**Cashier:** Dwadzieścia trzy złote. `(dva-JESH-cha tshih ZWO-teh)` - Twenty-three złoty.
**You:** Proszę. `(PRO-sheh)` - Here you go.

*You pay*

**Cashier:** Do widzenia! `(do vee-JEN-ya)` - Goodbye!
**You:** Do widzenia! `(do vee-JEN-ya)` - Goodbye!

**But it rarely goes this smoothly...**

---

## Complication 1: Loyalty Card Confusion

**Kaufland has the "K Karta" and "K Plus" app. Cashiers ask in different ways:**

**Version 1 - Just the name:**
- **Cashier:** K Plus? `(ka plooss)`
- **You:** Nie mam. `(nyeh mam)` - I don't have [it].

**Version 2 - Different phrasing:**
- **Cashier:** Karta K? `(KAR-ta ka)`
- **Cashier:** Aplikacja K Plus? `(ap-lee-KATS-ya ka plooss)`
- **Cashier:** Ma pan kartę? `(ma pan KAR-teh)` - Do you have a card?
- **You:** Nie, dziękuję. `(nyeh, jen-KOO-yeh)` - No, thank you.

**Version 3 - They ask about points:**
- **Cashier:** Punkty zbieramy? `(POONK-tih zbyeh-RA-mih)` - Collecting points?
- **You:** Nie. `(nyeh)` - No.

**What if you're not sure what they asked?**
- **You:** Przepraszam? `(psheh-PRA-sham)` - Sorry?
- **Cashier:** *Repeats or gestures*
- **You:** *If still confused* Nie mam. `(nyeh mam)` - I don't have [it]. *Covers most cases*

---

## Complication 2: Bag Questions (Multiple Versions)

**They'll ask about bags - but in different ways:**

**Version 1 - Just the word:**
- **Cashier:** Torba? `(TOR-ba)` - Bag?
- **You:** Tak. `(tak)` - Yes. OR: Nie, mam. `(nyeh, mam)` - No, I have [one].

**Version 2 - Plastic bag:**
- **Cashier:** Reklamówka? `(rek-la-MOOV-ka)`
- **Cashier:** Siatka? `(SHAT-ka)`
- *Same response*

**Version 3 - Size question:**
- **Cashier:** Duża czy mała? `(DOO-zhah chih MA-wah)` - Big or small?
- **You:** Mała. `(MA-wah)` - Small. OR: Duża. `(DOO-zhah)` - Big.

**Version 4 - How many:**
- **Cashier:** Ile toreb? `(EE-leh TO-reb)` - How many bags?
- **You:** Jedną. `(YED-noh)` - One. OR: Dwie. `(dvyeh)` - Two.

**If you have your own bag:**
- **You:** Mam własną. `(mam VWAS-noh)` - I have my own.
- **You:** *Show your bag* Mam. `(mam)` - I have [one].

## Complication 3: Price Seems Wrong

**The total is higher than expected.**

**Cashier:** Czterdzieści pięć złotych. `(chter-JESH-chee pyench ZWO-tih)` - Forty-five złoty.
**You:** *Expected 30 zł*

**Your options:**

**Option A - Ask to check:**
- **You:** Przepraszam, ile? `(psheh-PRA-sham, EE-leh)` - Sorry, how much?
- **Cashier:** *Repeats or points to screen*

**Option B - Question a specific item:**
- **You:** *Point to an item* To? Ile kosztuje? `(to? EE-leh kosh-TOO-yeh)` - This? How much?

**Option C - Ask them to check the price:**
- **You:** Może sprawdzić? `(MO-zheh SPRAV-jeech)` - Can you check?
- **Cashier:** *May void and re-scan, or check with supervisor*

**Option D - Remove an item:**
- **You:** To nie, dziękuję. `(to nyeh, jen-KOO-yeh)` - Not this, thanks.
- **Cashier:** *Removes it*

## Complication 4: Payment Problems

**Card terminal issues:**

**Cashier:** Terminal nie działa. `(ter-mee-NAL nyeh JA-wa)` - Terminal not working.
**Cashier:** Tylko gotówka. `(TIL-ko go-TOOF-ka)` - Cash only.

**Your options:**
- **You have cash:** Dobrze. `(DOB-zheh)` - Okay.
- **You don't have cash:** Nie mam gotówki. `(nyeh mam go-TOOF-kee)` - I don't have cash.
  - *You might need to leave your items or find an ATM*

**Contactless doesn't work:**
- **Cashier:** Proszę włożyć kartę. `(PRO-sheh VWO-zhich KAR-teh)` - Please insert the card.
- **You:** *Insert instead of tap*

**Card declined:**
- **Cashier:** Karta nie przeszła. `(KAR-ta nyeh PSHESH-wa)` - Card didn't go through.
- **Cashier:** Jeszcze raz? `(YESH-cheh raz)` - Try again?
- **You:** Dobrze. `(DOB-zheh)` - Okay. OR: Mam gotówkę. `(mam go-TOOF-keh)` - I have cash.

---

## Complication 5: They Ask For Exact Change

**Cashier:** Ma pan drobne? `(ma pan DROB-ne)` - Do you have small change?
**Cashier:** Drobne? `(DROB-ne)` - Small change?

**Your responses:**

**If you have coins:**
- **You:** Tak. `(tak)` - Yes. *Give coins*
- **You:** Ile? `(EE-leh)` - How much? *If you're not sure what they need*

**If you don't:**
- **You:** Nie mam. `(nyeh mam)` - I don't have [any].
- *They'll give you change from the register*

---

## Complication 6: Receipt Question

**Cashier:** Paragon? `(pa-RA-gon)` - Receipt?
**Cashier:** Paragon drukować? `(pa-RA-gon droo-KO-vach)` - Print the receipt?

**Your responses:**
- **You:** Tak. `(tak)` - Yes.
- **You:** Nie, dziękuję. `(nyeh, jen-KOO-yeh)` - No, thank you.

**If you need it for return/warranty:**
- Keep the receipt! You'll need it.

---

## Complication 7: Rapid-Fire Everything

**Sometimes they ask multiple things at once:**

**Cashier:** "DzieńdobryKartaTorbaGotówka?" *(sounds like one long word)*

**Your survival strategies:**

**Strategy 1 - Answer one thing at a time:**
- **You:** Nie. `(nyeh)` - No. *To the first thing*
- **Cashier:** *Moves to next question*

**Strategy 2 - Slow them down:**
- **You:** Wolniej proszę. `(VOL-nyey PRO-sheh)` - Slower please.

**Strategy 3 - The universal response:**
- **You:** Nie, dziękuję. `(nyeh, jen-KOO-yeh)` - No, thank you.
- *Works for most optional things*

## Cultural Notes

- **Pack fast**: Polish checkout is quick. Other customers are waiting. Pack your bags efficiently.
- **Bags cost money**: Plastic bags are paid (around 0,50 zł). Most Poles bring their own.
- **K Plus app**: Kaufland's loyalty program. Cashiers may ask even if you look confused.
- **Receipts are optional**: Many people decline. But keep it if you might return something.
- **Cash is still common**: Many older Poles pay cash. Having some zloty on hand is useful.

## Practice Variations

### Easiest Version
Memorize these three responses:
1. "Nie, dziękuję" - for any offer you don't want
2. "Nie mam" - for any card/thing you don't have
3. "Tak" / "Nie" - for simple yes/no questions

### Challenge Version
- Answer bag size: "Małą poproszę"
- Decline loyalty card with explanation: "Nie mam karty"
- If price seems wrong: "Może sprawdzić?"
