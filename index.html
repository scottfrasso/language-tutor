Why These Scenarios?

  • Realistic, not textbook - Multiple shopkeeper responses, upselling, mishearings
  • Living in Poland - Based on actual situations you'll encounter
  • Pronunciation focus - Phonetic guides for every Polish phrase
  • Cultural notes - Understand Polish customs and expectations
  • Progressive difficulty - From simple greetings to complex negotiations
  • Practical locations - Auchan, Kaufland, local bakeries, farmers markets

How to Use These Scenarios

  1. Choose your level - Start with A1 if you're a beginner
  2. Pick a situation - Choose something you'll encounter soon
  3. Study the vocabulary - Review the key words table
  4. Practice all variations - Don't just memorize one response - real life varies!
  5. Focus on pronunciation - Use the phonetic guides (CAPS = stress)
  6. Try it in real life - The best practice is actual conversation
